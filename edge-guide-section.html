Browser Guide

Getting the most out of
Microsoft Edge

Edge is your recommended browser for work. It's built to integrate seamlessly with Microsoft 365, so the more you use it, the smoother your day gets.

Step by step
What to do first
01

Sign in with your work account

Signing in keeps your browsing experience consistent and makes browser features work across your devices.

02

Pin the sites you use most

Add frequently visited systems and websites to your favourites so they're always a single click away.

03

Keep your browser tidy

Use tabs for active work, favourites for important sites, and close pages you no longer need.

04

Use help before raising a ticket

If something doesn't behave as expected, check the troubleshooting page first — it saves everyone time.

Top tips
Save the pages you use regularly as favourites for one-click access.
Keep only the tabs you need open — too many can slow things down.

💡

Microsoft's recommended troubleshooting steps

Microsoft's own support guidance recommends three things when problems arise: check for updates, restart Edge, and clear your browsing data. Running through these steps first resolves the majority of common browser issues.
